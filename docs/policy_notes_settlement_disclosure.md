# Settlement Disclosure Notes

## Scope

This memo frames the policy question correctly for the NPDB workflow.

## Key Point

The NPDB captures paid malpractice claims that must be reported under federal rules. That is different from whether settlement terms are publicly disclosed in court records or under state confidentiality practices.

## Implication

The notebook can analyze:

- settlement vs judgment shares
- payment levels by resolution type
- state-level variation in judgment rates
- Wisconsin's position relative to peer states

The notebook cannot, by itself, prove that state confidentiality rules cause those patterns.

## Interpretation Rule

If state differences appear, they should be framed as potentially reflecting:

- tort environment
- litigation incentives
- case mix
- reporting behavior

not as direct evidence about public disclosure law unless external legal research is added.
